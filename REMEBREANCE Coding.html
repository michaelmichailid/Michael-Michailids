World War 1

World War 1 also known as “the First World War and the Great War”. It began on July 28, 1914 and ended on November 11, 1918. It was the Allied powers vs the Central Powers. The main countries that were Allied Powers and that had fought was France, Russia and Britain. The Central Powers were Germany, Austria-Hungary, the Ottoman Empire and Bulgaria. The United States also fought along side with the Allies after 1917. Canada has been part of the first war when United Kingdom has entered the first world war by declaring war on Germany. The First World War was the bloodiest conflict in Canadian history, it has taking over 60,000 Canadians lives to save and to protect our country


World War 2

World War 2: 1939-1945. Over 45,000 Canadians dead. World War 2 was about Axis Powers vs Allied Powers. After the Axis Powers declared war which consisted (Germany, Italy and Japan), the Americans didn’t want to get involved but they joined with the Allies which consisted (The British Empire, the Soviet Union, France and China).
